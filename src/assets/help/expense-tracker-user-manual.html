AI Expense Tracker

User Manual

This guide walks users through receipts, budgets, categories, vendor rules, dashboards, and the AI copilot so they can use the app step by step without guessing where anything lives.

1. Getting Started

  1. Register your account and verify your email with OTP.
  2. Log in to open the main dashboard.
  3. Open Profile to complete your personal details and upload an avatar.

2. Dashboard

The dashboard is the command center. It shows budget health, top category, AI alerts, recurring-spend hints, charts, and the expense copilot chat.

  • Use the charts to understand monthly and category spending.
  • Read the AI guidance cards for risks and next actions.
  • Use the copilot chat for natural-language questions.

3. Receipts

  1. Open Receipts and choose a PDF or image file.
  2. Review the AI preview for vendor, amount, and category.
  3. Correct the category if needed and save the receipt.
  4. Use the filters and table to review older records.

4. Budgets

Budgets help the AI reason about risk and month-end forecasting.

  • Create category-based monthly budget rules.
  • Watch the budget advisor for projected spend and headroom.
  • Review the category watchlist to see which areas need attention.

5. Categories And Vendor Rules

  1. Create clean categories such as Groceries, Dining, Travel, or Housing.
  2. Add vendor rules for repeat merchants.
  3. Upload new receipts and confirm they auto-map correctly.

6. AI Copilot

The copilot can answer spending, subscription, receipt, and app usage questions from your real data.

Ask about risk Example: How much budget risk do I have right now?
Ask about subscriptions Example: Which subscriptions should I review first?
Ask for workflow help Example: Where do I add vendor rules?

7. Sample Data Pack

The app includes a sample receipt pack for groceries, dining, travel, housing, and subscriptions so the AI flows can be tested quickly.

8. Weekly Best Practice

  1. Upload the latest receipts.
  2. Correct any uncategorized items.
  3. Review vendor rules for repeat merchants.
  4. Check the budget watchlist and dashboard alerts.
  5. Ask the copilot for a summary and next actions.
